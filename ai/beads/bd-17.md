## Decision (operator)
Replace blank-line-paragraph anchoring with **sentence-level** anchoring for better provenance
granularity (the deferred bd-5 idea). This intentionally changes the anchor set, so the committed demo
example packs WILL change (and must be regenerated + committed). Approved by the operator.

## Fix (`packages/ingest/src/index.ts`)
- Keep paragraph blocks (split on blank lines) as the outer structure, then split each paragraph into
  **sentences**. Deterministic, documented heuristic: a boundary is a sentence-ending char `[.!?]`
  (allowing trailing quotes/brackets `["')\]]*`) followed by whitespace and then a non-whitespace
  char — or paragraph end. Decimals (`3.14`) don't split (punctuation not followed by whitespace).
  **Document the known limitation:** abbreviations (`e.g.`, `Dr.`) may over-split — acceptable heuristic,
  no over-engineering. Do NOT cross blank-line/paragraph boundaries.
- Each sentence span: trim leading/trailing whitespace to a tight span; `excerpt` = exact
  `normalized.slice(start,end)`; `id = anc-<start>-<end>`; `selector.kind="text_quote"`; offsets as
  strings; `extraction_method="native"`; `verifiable` from `verifyAnchor` (exact re-derivation). Skip
  empty/whitespace-only. Keep everything else (content hash, asset id, derivation, injectable clock,
  determinism) unchanged.
